ABOUT US

ABCD Pvt. Ltd. has set up five state of the art plants for meeting the ever growing demand. The company procures raw material directly from the centers of produce to maintain uniform taste and quality. The raw material is first cleaned, dried and tested with the help of special machines. It is then carefully grounded into the finished product passing through various stages.
Fully automatic machines have been installed for this process.
